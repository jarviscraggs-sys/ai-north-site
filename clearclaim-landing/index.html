The Problem

The Hidden Cost of Manual
Claim Management

Construction finance teams lose millions each year to duplicate claims, slow approvals and compliance mistakes. It doesn't have to be this way.

01

Duplicate & Re-claimed Work

The same work claimed twice across different cost codes, packages or claim periods — often undetected until it's too late.

02

Manual Checks & Delays

QS teams spending hours cross-referencing spreadsheets, previous claims and supporting documentation by hand — every single week.

03

Complex Compliance

VAT, CIS and DRC rules are constantly evolving. Getting them wrong means penalties, delays and damaged subcontractor relationships.

04

Poor Visibility

No single view across POs, work packages and cost codes. Finance teams are flying blind when approving payments worth hundreds of thousands.

Platform Features

Everything You Need.
Nothing You Don't.

Built specifically for UK construction finance — from the ground up.

Smart Submissions

Upload invoices and valuations with supporting attachments. Tag line items to cost codes, work packages and purchase orders automatically.

AI Duplicate Detection

Catches duplicates, re-claims, evidence reuse and over-claims before they're approved. Explainable alerts show exactly what was found and why.

QS Review & Approval

Built-in query workflow for QS teams. Raise, track and resolve queries. Approve, partially certify or reject with full commentary and evidence logging.

Flexible Structure

Model any project structure with cost codes, work packages and purchase orders. Supports complex multi-package contracts across unlimited subcontractors.

Compliance Engine

Automated VAT, CIS and Domestic Reverse Charge checks built into every approval. Stay compliant with HMRC rules without the manual overhead.

Audit & History

Immutable log of every submission, change, approval, rejection and query. Timestamped, user-attributed and exportable for disputes or external audits.

AI Intelligence

AI That Thinks Like
a Quantity Surveyor

Our AI doesn't just pattern-match. It understands construction claims — the language, the structure, the evidence — and flags what a seasoned QS would question.

Text & Numeric Similarity

Detects the same work described differently across claims. "Structural beams Level 3" and "L3 steel beam installation" — our AI recognises them as one.

Document Fingerprinting

Flags when photos, PDFs and other supporting evidence have been reused across different claims — a common fraud vector caught automatically.

Explainable Alerts

Every flag comes with a side-by-side comparison and clear reasoning. QS teams can make informed decisions — not just dismiss alerts blind.
